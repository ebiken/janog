= Switch ASIC / NPU Quick Reference
:toc:
:toclevels: 2

Quick reference for Switch ASICs and NPUs supporting 100Gbps to 1.6Tbps interfaces.
Covers Broadcom StrataXGS/StrataDNX families and NVIDIA Spectrum family.

Abbreviations: NRZ = Non-Return-to-Zero, PAM4 = Pulse-Amplitude Modulation 4-level,
CPO = Co-Packaged Optics, SerDes = Serializer/Deserializer, ToR = Top of Rack,
SP = Service Provider, DC = Data Center, HBM = High Bandwidth Memory,
FlexE = Flexible Ethernet, RoCE = RDMA over Converged Ethernet.
